FAQ

Common Questions

Can I write for Synet? +

Yes! We're always open to pitches from writers with a background in marine science, environmental journalism, or ocean-adjacent fields. Send us a brief pitch via the contact form above — include your topic idea, angle, and a writing sample.

How is Synet funded? +

Synet is currently self-funded and independent. All content is free to read. We may explore reader support and ethical sponsorships in the future, but our editorial independence is non-negotiable.

I found a factual error. What should I do? +

Please tell us immediately — accuracy is our top priority. Use the contact form, select "Correction / Feedback," and include the article title and the specific claim you believe is incorrect. We'll investigate and correct promptly with a transparent editor's note.

Can I republish Synet content? +

All Synet content is protected by copyright. Republication requires written permission. Educational, non-commercial use with proper attribution may be permitted — contact us to discuss.
